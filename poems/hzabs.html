Headless Zombies And Brainless Specters
They'll bleed to go out of their way
Just to harm our society
We know you can have the rights
But must you over express them?
Do we really need assemblies and conventions
At the most unfit times?
Must we keep seven arms around
Just to feel secure?
Does this society yearn to hold its holy values?
Surely I say not for its the truth.
In God we trust, or the money?
Conspiracies can't flee for long
And the underlying messages of advertising.
Why won't people analyze what they sense?
Do people not question the understood truth?
Or they do, but won't say anything?
This country has planted microcomputers in our heads
Without getting near us.
There's a reason why you see the media the way you do.
And this unbalance of power challenged.
Are we free or more oppressed?
Are we brave or more of a coward?
Is this the land? Maybe.
Is this our time? Surely.
Is this the end? Absolutely.
