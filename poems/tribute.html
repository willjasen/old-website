Tribute To An Angel
Whether real or just an illusion
In your hands, it becomes confusion.

Whether the truth or merely a lie
From you fears, you do not hide.

Whether honest or telling a fib,
You're the one who makes magic live.

Whether on the street or stage or in restraints
I speak for many when I say thanks.
