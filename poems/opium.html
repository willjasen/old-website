Opium Of The Masses
This chaos that spreads so blindly.
Mass hysteria encompassing the people.
It also incapicitates them, too.
Do they not realize their weaknesses?
Why do they have the need
To tell me how to live my life.
Would not opposing beliefs face off
To both say what is just and right
Only to find out one can win,
We all perceive what we view differently
Which makes us all unique.
But why must we have a mainstream belief
Especially one of a false truth.
Do they not praise what they made?
Only to scorn those of unlikedness?
This only leads me to believe one thing.
They obviously are all hypocrits.
And what they think must not be true.
Which also leads me to assume
They don't want the truth either.
The truth will always set you free,
But sometimes,
It will piss you off first.
