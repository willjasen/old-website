The Neighborhood Of Fake Believe
Once upon a time
In a land far away
There was a kingdom
But no sun that day.

King Friday thought money has happiness
And he thought he needed more
He knew he couldn't steal it
So he began to tax the poor.

Queen Sara was tired of her husband
And the way he would never care
She left him for another man
And no sun still, just fair.

When Prince Tuesday heard the news
He couldn't help but to cry
He took to many pills and overdosed
And then he died.

It was a sad time
And when Henrietta Pussycat heard the news
She thought to herself
That the kingdom was doomed.

And X the Owl's house burned down
And the fire gave him no slack
But because of the heavy tax
He couldn't build it back.

Lady Elaine was getting very lonely
And stayed inside her hosue to think
She never would come out
So she was labeled a freak.

But Daniel Tiger's story is not great
The king thought he was a spy
So the king tortured him alot
And eventually, his head went bye-bye.

Their stories were all tragic
As you can clearly see
Maybe there was no pretend
In the neighborhood of make believe.
