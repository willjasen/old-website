Just You
Your warmth and touch is like a raging inferno throughout a desolate forest with burning cinders twirling around.

Yet it's so calm and soothing like crisp, clean, cold water flowing over the smooth rocks of a creek bed.

Your stare is so intimate and allow passage into amystika where untold stories hide and are daring to creep out.

Your presence is a blessing of Lucifer and is made to make me feel safe, while excited also.

Your words are unspoken fruits of which I yearn to feast upon because I am famished of the dirt and blood being fed to me now.

Your smile is a drug and addicting. It's persuasiveness is cunning, yet while ever so shy, but leads me into the trap of which you've placed for me.

Your character is one of wit and I cannot help to ponder why no one has valued this in you yet.

Just you.
